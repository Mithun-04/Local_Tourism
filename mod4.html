Keys Prima By Lemon Tree Hotels, Thekkady:

Description: Keys Prima by Lemon Tree Hotels, Thekaddy is strategically located near Kumily, a gateway town into Kerala from Tamil Nadu. It is close to the Periyar National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary (PNP), India's largest wildlife sanctuary. The name Thekaddy is derived from the word ‘thekku’ which means teak. The sanctuary is famous for its dense evergreen, semi-evergreen, moist, deciduous forests and savanna grasslands.
